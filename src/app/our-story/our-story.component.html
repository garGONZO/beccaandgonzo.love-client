How We Met
We met in 2009 at MIT. We were both interested in chemistry and biology and took many classes together. In 2013, Becca graduated with a degree in Chemistry while Gonzo graduated with a degree in Biological Engineering.
The Proposal
We went to Denver for vacation. We spent the day geeking out at the Museum of Nature and Science. The last stop of the tour was the rooftop where the Denver skyline set the perfect scene. Gonzo got down on one knee where he declared his undying love. Through tears, Becca said yes! We celebrated the night with a tour of the city by horse-drawn carriage and dinner at a French restaurant.
